Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how AgriTech uses cookies and similar storage to keep the platform secure, remember preferences, and improve your experience.

Effective May 2026 Preference storage Essential functionality first

What we store

Theme, language, session, and consent choices may be saved in your browser.

Why we use it

Cookies and local storage help keep you signed in and make the site easier to use.

Your control

You can manage or delete browser data through your settings at any time.

1. What Cookies and Storage We Use

AgriTech uses essential cookies and browser storage to support core site features. Depending on your device and browser, this may include:

  • Session cookies for authentication and secure navigation.
  • Preference storage for theme, language, and accessibility choices.
  • Consent storage to remember whether you accepted or limited the cookie banner.
  • Analytics or performance storage, if enabled, to help us understand page usage.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • To keep you signed in and maintain secure sessions across pages.
  • To remember settings so the platform feels consistent on return visits.
  • To support product feedback, marketplace interactions, and feature improvements.
  • To measure technical performance and fix issues more efficiently.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some embedded tools or external services may place their own cookies or use similar technologies. Those services operate under their own privacy and cookie policies.

When possible, AgriTech limits third-party tracking to what is necessary for platform functionality and reliability.

4. Your Choices

You can accept or limit cookies through the consent banner, and you can also manage them in your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect login, preferences, or other features.

  • Accept all cookies for the full experience.
  • Use essential-only storage to limit non-essential tracking.
  • Clear cookies and local storage whenever you want through your browser controls.

5. Updates and Contact

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect technical or legal changes. The latest version will always appear on this page.
