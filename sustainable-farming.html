Daily Sustainability Tips

Simple practices you can implement today

Morning Routine

Check soil moisture levels before irrigation. Use the finger test or install moisture sensors for accurate readings.

Composting

Turn your organic waste into nutrient-rich compost. Mix green and brown materials in a 3:1 ratio for optimal results.

Natural Pest Control

Plant marigolds, basil, and mint around your crops. These natural repellents keep harmful insects away.

Waste Reduction

Reuse plastic containers for seedling trays and water collection. Every small action contributes to sustainability.

Solar Energy

Consider solar panels for irrigation pumps. Initial investment pays off through reduced electricity bills.

Data Tracking

Keep records of your sustainable practices. Track water usage, soil health, and cost savings over time.
