Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how AgriTech collects, uses, shares, and protects data across the marketplace, advisory tools, and AI-powered services.

Effective May 2026 Cookies and local storage No sale of personal data

AgriTech is designed to help farmers, buyers, and service providers connect safely. We keep data collection limited to what is needed for account management, marketplace interactions, and agricultural assistance.

Data minimization

We only collect information needed to operate the platform and improve core services.

User control

You can manage preferences, request access, and ask us to delete supported data where applicable.

Responsible sharing

Data is shared only with trusted providers, legal authorities, or with your consent.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to provide AgriTech services and keep accounts secure.

  • Account details such as name, email, phone number, and profile preferences.
  • Marketplace content, support requests, feedback, and other information you choose to submit.
  • Crop images, advisory inputs, and similar files uploaded for AI-assisted analysis.
  • Technical data such as device type, browser, IP address, session logs, and language or theme settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide marketplace browsing, recommendations, and communication between users.
  • To process AI-based agricultural insights, including crop and disease-related guidance.
  • To authenticate accounts, remember preferences, and keep sessions secure.
  • To diagnose bugs, measure performance, and improve product reliability.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. We may share information only when it is necessary to operate the service, comply with the law, or protect users and the platform.

  • Trusted providers that help us host, store, secure, or analyze platform data.
  • Authorities or legal representatives when disclosure is required by law or to protect rights and safety.
  • Business transfers if the platform is reorganized, merged, or acquired, subject to the same protections.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies and local storage to remember preferences such as language, theme, and session state, and to make the site easier to use.

Our Terms of Service explain broader platform rules, while this page focuses on privacy and data handling.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect the information we process. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to reduce risk and keep credentials protected.

6. Data Retention

We keep information only for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and support legitimate business needs.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain data, as well as object to processing or withdraw consent where applicable.

  • You can update account preferences and clear browser storage used by the site.
  • You can contact us to request access or deletion of your information where supported.
  • You can limit cookies through browser settings, though some features may stop working.

8. Children's Privacy

AgriTech is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a child has submitted information, contact us so we can review and remove it.

9. Policy Updates and Contact

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page, and continued use of the platform means you accept the updated policy.
